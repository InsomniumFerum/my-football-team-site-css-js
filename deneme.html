About

The team, which was founded in 2010, officially took its place on the football fields in 2012 by Furkan Çat. Although he played in the third league of Ukraine in his first years, he was promoted to the 2nd league in 3 years and achieved great success in a short time.

Subsequently, this success attracted the attention of the 1st League teams and they offered F.C Kharkov players unimaginable fees and transferred the players from the team one by one with their testimonials. FC today. Only a few of the Khavkov players are actively continuing their football life.
